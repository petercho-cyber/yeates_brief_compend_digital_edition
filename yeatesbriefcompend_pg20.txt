
Decision of Controverses
Book 2.
Cap.18
How Rights & Obligations cease.
Obligations cease by three several Ways.
1st. By the Paying or performing what was
due 2d. By Remission in favour of
the Debtor 3d. & by failing of the
Condition
Cap. 19 How Controversies are to be decided in
a state of natural Liberty
In natural Liberty Controversies are
best decided by friendly conferences of
the Parties, or the Interposal of com-
mon friends; or by an absolute Com-
promise or Submission to Arbiters of
approved Characters.
